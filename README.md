# NEXUS

**The RE/MAX hub for Costa Rica & Central America.**

NEXUS modernizes how buyers discover properties and how agents, brokers, and regional owners consume operational data — replacing fragmented tools, broken search pages, and monthly Excel reports with a single intelligent platform.

---

## What Is NEXUS?

A four-layer platform built on top of existing RE/MAX CCA office APIs:

| Layer | User | Core Value |
|-------|------|------------|
| **Buyer** | Property seekers | Tinder-style swipe discovery, preference-based matching, co-buyer collaboration |
| **Agent** | RE/MAX agents | Client interest visibility, CRM, buyer-listing matching engine, lead management |
| **Broker** | Office brokers | Office-wide performance dashboards, agent analytics, conversion insights |
| **Region** | Regional owners | Multi-office overview, portfolio health, market trends |

## Business Model

Freemium — gifted to the entire RE/MAX Costa Rica network.

- **Free:** Full data transparency for all offices. Buyers get the complete swipe experience. Agents see who's interested in their listings.
- **Paid:** Productivity tools, intelligence, and advanced analytics — CRM pipeline, proactive matching engine, broker dashboards, trend analysis.

> Free = see your data. Paid = act on it faster.
